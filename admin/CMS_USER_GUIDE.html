User Manual

Welcome to your creative dashboard. This guide will help you manage your shop, tell your story, and engage with your community effortlessly.

🔐 1. Secure Access

Your site uses a "Double-Gate" security system to protect your data and your customer's experience.

Step 1: The Admin Wall

When you go to /admin, you'll first see a browser login box. This is powered by Cloudflare.

  • 1
    Username: Use the admin email provided during setup.
    Password: Enter your secure admin password.

⚠️ Security Lockout

If you enter the wrong password 5 times, you will be locked out for 10 minutes. This protects against unauthorized access.

Step 2: The GitHub Handshake

Once past the wall, you'll see a "Login with GitHub" button. This connects the dashboard to the site's files.

  • 2
    Click the button. If you're not logged into GitHub, it will ask for your GitHub credentials.
  • 3
    Authorize the application. Once done, the dashboard will load your content.

🛍️ 2. Shop Settings — Products

This is where you manage your inventory. Every product you add here reflects instantly on your live site.

Essential Fields

  • Title & Slug: The title is what customers see. The slug is for the URL (e.g., ocean-journal).
  • Pricing: Set the original price. If you add a Sale Price, the site automatically shows the "Strikethrough" effect.
  • Stock Status: Choose between In Stock, Pre-Order, or Sold Out. Selecting "Sold Out" automatically disables the "Add to Cart" button.

Decorative Badges

  • Sales Badge: Shown next to the price (e.g., "SALE", "SOLD OUT").
  • Promo Badge: A decorative ribbon in the corner (e.g., "Best Seller", "New Arrival").
  • Curator's Pick: Toggle this on to feature the product in the special section on the homepage.

💡 The "Multiples of 10" Sorting Hack

When setting the Sort Order, always use multiples of 10 (10, 20, 30...). If you want to insert a new product later between 10 and 20, you can just label it 15 without having to re-number everything else!

The "What's Inside" Section

You can add specific features for each product. Each feature needs an Icon Name. We use Google Material Symbols.

Examples: auto_awesome (Sparkles), favorite (Heart), palette (Painter's Palette).

🏷️ 3. Category Builder

Categories organize your products into collections. Each category can have its own dedicated landing page.

Setting Up a Category

  • Slug: This determines the URL (e.g., stickers results in yaaazzcreative.com/stickers/).
  • Icon: Select an icon from the dropdown to represent the category in menus.
  • Landing Page: If Enable Landing Page is true, the site creates a beautiful full-screen "sheet" for that category.

🏠 4. Homepage Design

Your homepage is your storefront. You can change every piece of text and every image through the 🏠 Homepage Settings.

Hero Section

Control the main headline, subtitle, and the two floating hero images. You can even change the Handwritten Floating Text (e.g. "Collect every single scrap!").

Why Our Collection

Add features that make your brand unique. You can have 1 "Large" feature (which spans more space) and several "Small" ones.

"Spotted in the Wild"

This is your testimonial section. Add quotes from your fans, their social handles, and a photo of them using your products.

🙏 5. Devotionals & Club Hub

These sections are for community engagement and special drops.

Automated Prompts

The site comes with a year-round list of 12 monthly prompts. You can find these in 🙏 Devotionals Settings.

Overriding: If you want to set a special prompt for the CURRENT month, fill out the Prompt title (Month) and Prompt quote fields. Leave them blank to go back to the automated schedule.

Sticker Hacks & Layouts

In the 🎨 Club Hub Settings, you can manage the "Creative Layouts" carousel. Add a title, a short description, and an image for each creative tip you want to share.

⚙️ 6. Store Operations

Managing the "back-office" parts of your shop.

🛒 Cart & Checkout

  • GCash: Re-upload your QR code image anytime to update your payment method.
  • Shipping: Set your Flat Rate Shipping (currently PHP 120) and the email where order notifications are sent.

📦 Shipping Policies

  • Update your delivery timelines for Local vs. International shipping.
  • Manage your "Return Policy" bullet points and "Lost Parcel" notes here.

🧭 Navigation Menu

Found in General Settings. Add new menu items, link them to categories, and set the Active ID to the category slug so the link underlines when visited.

🛠️ 7. Troubleshooting

"I saved my changes but they aren't on the site!"

Because your site is a static site (super fast and secure), it needs a few minutes to "build" after you click Save. Usually, changes appear within 2–3 minutes.

"Image previews aren't working."

Ensure your images are .jpg, .png, or .webp. Extremely large files (over 5MB) may take longer to process in the dashboard. Try to keep product photos around 1000px wide for the best balance of quality and speed.
