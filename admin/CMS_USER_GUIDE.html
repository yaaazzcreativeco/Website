A comprehensive walkthrough for managing your products, visuals, and site experience with ease.

🛍️ 1. Managing Products

Your store’s heart is the Shop Settings — Products collection. This is where you bring your creations to life.

Essential Configuration

  • Title & Slug: Use descriptive titles. Slugs should be lowercase and hyphenated (e.g., humble-roots-journal).
    • \n
  • Pricing: Input numbers only. If you set a \"Sale Price\", the site automatically marks down the original price for you.
  • Status: Use In Stock for regular sales, Pre-Order for upcoming drops, and Sold Out to temporarily disable purchasing.

🔢 The Logic of Sort Order

Manual sorting gives you the power to curate your layout. The site sorts numerically from lowest to highest.

\n
\n

Pro Sorting Hack

\n

Instead of numbering 1, 2, 3, use multiples of 10 (10, 20, 30). If you add a new \"Best Seller\" later, you can simply label it 15 to place it exactly where you want without moving every other product.
